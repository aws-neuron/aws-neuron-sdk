=======================================
Neuron Profiler (archived)
=======================================

.. warning::

   This content is archived. The **Neuron Profiler** (``neuron-profile``) and
   **Neuron Profiler 2.0 (Beta)** reached end of support in Neuron 2.29. They
   are provided here for historical reference only and are not maintained.

   For current profiling, analysis, and optimization workflows, use
   :ref:`Neuron Explorer <neuron-explorer-home>`. Migration guidance lives in
   the :ref:`Neuron Explorer FAQ <neuron-explorer-faq>`.

The pages below captured the final documented behavior of ``neuron-profile``
and Neuron Profiler 2.0 (Beta). They may reference APIs, environment
variables, and workflows that no longer apply to current Neuron releases.
